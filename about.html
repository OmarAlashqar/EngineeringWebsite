I'm a student, a renowned try hard, and a bit of a dreamer. My hobbies include reading, drawing, designing, and coding. This website is the first step to a future in software engineering, my dream career path.

I've realized that my passion lies in the world of coding when, in 10th Grade, I joined my school's Robotics club. It introduced me to a whole new world that I never knew was available to kids like me.

Computer Engineering has been an awesome course, both entertaining and educational! Since I'm hoping to get into Software Engineering, the skills I learned throughout this course will be applied in all aspects.

I foresee nights staying up trying to optimize circuits or trying to debug an annoying piece of code. It might not appeal to most people, but I like challenging myself!
